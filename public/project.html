Frenzy

dynamic social media app with trending feeds,
category, exploration and real time streaming features.

project overview

frenzy is a modern social app with designed to keep user engaged with content of their own interest and connect them with people of like interest, it features a dedicated stream seection , category navigator and a search funtionality.

Key Features

-intuitive tab navigation (For You, Following, Trending,)
-Real-time search and recommendation
-Frenzy stream for live content
-category navigator for quick topic switching
